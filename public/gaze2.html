Alzheimer’s Assessment Instructions

Welcome to the Alzheimer's Assessment. This assessment is designed to help identify early signs of cognitive decline.

Here’s what you need to know before starting:

  • Duration: The entire process will take approximately 10-15 minutes.
  • Equipment: Ensure your device has a functioning webcam and a stable internet connection.
  • Environment: Choose a quiet and well-lit place to complete the assessment without interruptions.
  • Instructions: Follow the on-screen instructions carefully. If you encounter any issues, you can restart the assessment at any time.
  • Privacy: Your data is secure and confidential. It will only be used for assessment purposes.

Once you are ready, click the button below to begin the assessment.
